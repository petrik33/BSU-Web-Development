Project Management and Development

About the Project System

Our platform "Advanced Technology Development" is designed for managing projects in the field of information technology. The system enables customers to create projects with detailed descriptions of tasks and required resources, while managers analyze and allocate them among teams of developers. The main functionality includes project formation, team assignment, and resource control.

The platform features the ability to search and filter developer teams, providing flexible personnel management. It also provides tools to view project information for specific customers, team members of developers, and details about developers working on a particular project. Additional functions involve managing developer assignments to projects and controlling invoice payments.
